love is romantic but profound

Created2024-06-02|Updated2024-06-02
|Post Views:

Love is not only romantic but more profound.

Gathering for happiness is not from love.

Separating for individuality is not from love.

Love is together, seeking happiness from tolerance and delayed satisfaction.

Love is apart, suffering nostalgia from reflection and upgraded personality.
